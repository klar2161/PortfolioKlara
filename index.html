About Me

This is me

Ahoj! My name is Klara Nemcikova and I am from the Czech Republic. Currently, I am studying bachelor programme - Web Development in Esbjerg. As a person, I would describe myself as productive, easy-going and positive. I consider my detail-oriented mind as a strength because I keep as many details in mind as I can. At the same time, I know when to look at the project from far away and focus on the whole picture.

Creativity is simply in my nature and it follows even in my everyday life. From replacing kitchen gadgets that I don't own for some specific tasks in my kitchen to the structure, look and flow of my projects. I have a curious mind so I always seek new and unknown. Educating myself is one of the most important values in my life because I don't like stereotypes and limited points of view. I believe that it is important to be able to work individually same as in the group. Mutual respect, exchange of ideas and then the following brainstorming are the keys to the best result.

My biggest passion is design and developing web applications. Therefore I want to work as a Web developer preferably if there would be a small part of designing as well.
